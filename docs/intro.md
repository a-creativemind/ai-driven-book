# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## From Foundations to Embodied Intelligence

Welcome to this comprehensive textbook on **Physical AI** and **Humanoid Robotics**. This book bridges the gap between theoretical artificial intelligence and its physical manifestation in the real world through robotic systems.

## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that:

- **Interact** with the physical world through sensors and actuators
- **Learn** from embodied experiences rather than just data
- **Adapt** to real-world uncertainties and constraints
- **Operate** in unstructured environments alongside humans

Unlike traditional AI that operates purely in the digital realm, Physical AI must contend with the messiness of reality: friction, noise, delays, and the fundamental unpredictability of physical interactions.
