جسمانی مصنوعی ذہانت سے مراد وہ مصنوعی ذہانت کے نظام ہیں جو:

This textbook is organized into five parts:

Explore the fundamental concepts that distinguish physical AI from traditional AI:

- **Embodied Intelligence**: Why physical form matters for intelligence
- **Sensors & Actuators**: The interfaces between AI and the physical world
- **Control Systems**: Making robots move with precision and stability
- **Sim-to-Real Transfer**: Bridging the gap between simulation and reality

- **Kinematics & Dynamics**: The mathematics of robot motion
- **Bipedal Locomotion**: The art and science of walking
- **Dexterous Manipulation**: Grasping and manipulating objects
- **Robot Perception**: Seeing and understanding the world

### Part III: Learning Systems

Understand how robots learn from experience:

- **Reinforcement Learning**: Learning through trial and error
- **Imitation Learning**: Learning from demonstrations
- **Foundation Models**: Large-scale AI for robotics

### Part IV: Tooling & Labs

Get hands-on experience with industry-standard tools:

- **ROS 2**: The Robot Operating System

### Part V: Ethics & Future

Consider the broader implications:

- **Safety**: Ensuring robots don't harm humans
- **Alignment**: Building robots that share human values
- **Human-Robot Interaction**: Designing collaborative systems
